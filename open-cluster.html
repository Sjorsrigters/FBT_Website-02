Open Cluster
Poster
Year: 2021
Category: Commisioned
Commissioned by: The graphic design department of the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague
In collaboration with: Ralph Bruens
Together with graphic designer Ralph Bruens we made a poster for the thesis launch of the fourth year graphic design department students of the Royal Academy of Art in The Hague.

An open cluster is a group of up to a few thousand stars that were formed from the same giant molecular cloud and have roughly the same age. This would, to some extent, represent the students during their time of studying together. This year the presentation took place in cyberspace, on a zoom call to be more precise. During the presentation, students could read parts of their thesis and watch their video essays together that were based on their theses.
