Contemporary Flavours
Exhibition identity
Type of project: Self-initiated
Held at: The Royal Academy of Art, The Hague
Together with graphic designers Ralph Bruens and Merel Meijer we created an event called Contemporary Flavours at the Royal Academy of Art in The Hague.

Contemporary Flavours was an event where students from 2nd and 3rd year graphic design could show there IST projects (Individual Study Track (IST) that focuses on the discovery and development of student fascinations and preferences and their personal artistic attitude). For Contemporary Flavours we created the identity and took care of the organisation of the event.
