How it Really Happend
Publication
Year: 2019
Category: Commissioned (school assignment)
Commissioned by: Bart de Baets
For an assignment from graphic designer Bart de Baets, we as a class had to redesign a book about mythology. Together, all of these pages would make the publication titled, 'How It Really Happened'.

Sjors Rigters got the first pages of the mythology book and decided to collect different images of eggs, since the egg is often seen as a symbol for the beginning of life. By pulling these images out of context and bringing them together again, the combinations form new relationships and a visual narrative.

Fabiola van den Berg took one sentence from the original publication that was presented: 'God of light Helios saw everything and knew everything'. It made her think of how we perceive celebrities nowadays, seeing that sometimes it seems like we don't think of them as real people. Searching up the weirdest things about celebrities can be seen as an example of their 'inhumanness'. The result of these pages is a collection of such google searches in combination with the original images from the mythology book.
