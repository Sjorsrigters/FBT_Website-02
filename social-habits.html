Social Habits
Publication
Year: 2019
Type of project: Self-initiated
Exhibited at: In the Future... | The Gray Space in the Middle, The Hague (2019)
A publication made for a company that will exist in 2083. This company is called Social Habits. In the year 2021 we are already experience the feeling of being watched by some of the world's (relatively) recent technological advances, such as our smartphone.

In the year 2083, Social Habits (and companies alike) will know everything you do everyday, even the amount of times you sneeze. At the end of each day, they will print you a receipt with a time coded list of everything you did that day. This publication is a visual representation of this future company.
