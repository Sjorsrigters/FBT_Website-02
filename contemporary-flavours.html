Contemporary Flavours
Exhibition identity
Year: 2020
Type of project: Self-initiated
Event location: Royal Academy of Art, The Hague
In collaboration with: Ralph Bruens and Merel Meijer
Together with graphic designers Ralph Bruens and Merel Meijer we created an event called Contemporary Flavours at the Royal Academy of Art in The Hague.

Contemporary Flavours was an event where second and third year graphic design students could show their IST projects. Individual Study Track (IST) focuses on the discovery and development of the fascinations and preferences of students and their personal artistic attitudes). For Contemporary Flavours, we created the identity and took care of the organisation of the event.
