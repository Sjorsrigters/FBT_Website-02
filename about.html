What we do
Founded by Two offers creative direction and graphic design. We create and realise concepts, research being an intrinsic part of our concepts and we produce many different types of media such as publications, posters, websites, videos and installations for clients in both the cultural and commercial field.

Together we approach each project as a new story to tell, with a desire to develop bold visual languages and profound design solutions, always adapting to the needs of the specific project. Our process-driven work often consists of extensive (visual) research, a feeling for image-making and use of some serendipity once in a while, with the use of a great variety of techniques and programs.


Who we are
Founded by Two is a graphic design studio based in Rotterdam founded by Fabiola van den Berg and Sjors Rigters. We are two Dutch graphic designers who graduated in the summer of 2021 from the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague.

Together we have studied graphic design for “sixteen years” and because of this we have become native speakers in visual language. While studying together we found out that we share the same passion for design and typography. In the end, we are two friends who started a studio together, who love playing chess and who experience love at first sight upon spotting beautifully designed publications.
